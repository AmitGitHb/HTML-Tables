The SANKHLA ENTERPRISES, warehouse was estd. in the year 2022. It has total storage capacity of 4700 MT. It is situated at the distance of 12 KMs from the main Suratgarh city, in the village of Piperan, on the National Highway- NH-62.

Currently, it is storing wheat grain, supplied from the the Food Corporation of India.

It also has the ESSAE Computerised Static WeighBridge of capacity 150 Tons. Its one of its kind in the area because, it has the latest Model of Load Cell and Cemented Platform.
